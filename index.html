About me

I am Programmer, and an Entreprenuer. My Entrepreneurial journey from ground zero. From selling Digital CDs to creating sustainable blockchain and AI Solutions. My journey by far has been amazing.

When I was 13, I stumbled through a phase "Everyone has a different perception of life, If your's different? Prove it to the world".This ignited me and hence the journey of creating something better started since then.Many of my Startups failed. Every startup I did, has a lesson to learn.

 I have worked and used:
JavaScript, NodeJS, ReactJS, React Native, MongoDB, PHP, CodeIgniter, Laravel, MySQL, Socket.io, Firebase, AWS, GCP, Docker, Kubernetes, Ansible, Solidity, Etherium, Python, Keras, Pandas, Jupyter Notebooks. I love Open Source. 
Currently working as CEO and Co-Founder at ProCohat Technologies, Lead Organizer at Google Developer Group
